# Task 2 – Student Digital Portfolio Reflection

## Platforms Set Up

For this task, I chose to set up accounts on **GitHub**, **LinkedIn**, and **HackerRank** — three platforms that directly align with my goals as a B.Tech student in Cybersecurity at VIT Bhopal.

### GitHub
GitHub is the world's largest code hosting platform, used by developers and students worldwide to store, version-control, and showcase their projects. I created a profile README repository (a special repo named after my username) that includes my name, branch, academic year, and a brief note about my interest in cybersecurity and digital forensics. GitHub will be my primary portfolio platform throughout my degree.

### LinkedIn
LinkedIn is the world's leading professional networking platform, widely used by recruiters, internship coordinators, and industry professionals. I filled in my Education section with my B.Tech (CSE – Cybersecurity) degree, VIT Bhopal as the institution, and my expected graduation year. LinkedIn will help me connect with professionals and apply for internships from my second year onwards.

### HackerRank
HackerRank is a competitive programming and skill-testing platform used by companies for technical hiring assessments. I created an account and completed a beginner-level challenge to get familiar with the interface. Over the next four years, I plan to build a strong HackerRank profile by earning domain badges in Python, Problem Solving, and eventually Security-focused challenges.

### Future Plans
Over my four years at VIT Bhopal, I plan to: commit projects regularly to GitHub (especially cybersecurity tools and forensics scripts), grow my LinkedIn network with faculty, seniors, and industry contacts, and steadily climb HackerRank difficulty levels to prepare for campus placements and GATE.
